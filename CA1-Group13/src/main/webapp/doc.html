Inheritance Strategy:

The Strategy of our inheritance: The Person and Company classes inherit from InfoEntity, so Person and Company will extend the id and email attribute from the InfoEntity class. We have make the InfoEntity class as a abstract class, because it us the methods from Person and Company, and still have abstract methods that Person and Company does extends.
